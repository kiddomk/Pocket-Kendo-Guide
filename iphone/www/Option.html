About Us

Kendo Pocket Guide is developed by Jun Seki using JQuery Mobile & PhoneGap. It provides reference to Kendo waza and kata.

More apps from FarEastGadget.com will be coming soon.

FarEastGadget.com is founded by Jun Seki, who is an tech entrepreneur and .NET/Web developer.

FarEastGadget.com is a tech startup company which provides the latest mobile apps and digital gadgets from far east (Greater China, Japan, Korea) area. The platform also exchanges information & ideas between European nations and Asian countries. Based in London where the company gains access to a vast range of enrepreneurship events and supports.
